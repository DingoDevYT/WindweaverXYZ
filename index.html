About Windweaver

Windweaver follows a duck who loses his flight after a devastating event sees him lose his ability to fly. With what wind based ability he has left, he must travel across new lands - using new abilities found throughout each level.

This high-speed action platformer focuses on momentum and slope physics, where players chain wind abilities with the environment to create powerful combos and blitz through levels. Collect power-ups, use environmental elements, and build speed across varied landscapes in a quest to reclaim the skies and restore his lost flight through fast-paced platforming action.

Game Features

Dynamic Ability Switching

Switch between different wind abilities mid-level to adapt to challenges. Master the art of seamless transitions between powers to maintain momentum and flow.

Windsurfing Mechanics

Ride the wind currents and surf across diverse terrains. Use environmental wind patterns to propel yourself through levels at breakneck speeds.

Living Towns & NPCs

Explore vibrant towns filled with NPCs offering dialogue, shops for upgrades, and missions to expand your journey. Each location tells part of your story.

Multiplayer Adventures

Team up with friends in cooperative multiplayer modes. Share the journey and combine your abilities to overcome challenges together.

Momentum-Based Physics

Experience fluid movement with slope physics that reward skillful play. Chain abilities and environmental interactions to build incredible speed.

Power-ups & Upgrades

Collect power-ups throughout your journey and visit shops to upgrade your abilities. Customize your playstyle and become the ultimate Windweaver.
