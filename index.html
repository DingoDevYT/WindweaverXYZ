About Windweaver

You are a Windweaver — once a guardian of the skies, now stripped of your full power. Cast from the heights, you must begin the long journey back to the Isle of Wind, relying only on the faint power that remains within you. Every step matters. Every jump demands precision. By bending the wind still at your command, you’ll run faster, leap farther, and cling to walls as you scale your way through a world of environments, obstacles, and pathways.

This high-speed action platformer has a direct emphasise on fluid movement and slope physics, where players will chain their abilities with environmental elements to build up incredible speed and execute spectacular combos to blitz through levels. Experience the thrill of windsurfing down mountain slopes, wall-running through ancient ruins, and soaring through the air as keep running to restore your lost flight - and maybe even discover the truth of your world as you meet new friends along the way!

Master Your Abilities

Combine your base movements with collectible ability shards in leveles to create powerful combos and navigate challenging terrain with style, speed and skill.

Dynamic Ability System

Equip two abilities simultaneously and swap between them instantly to create powerful combinations.

Use your current ability, swap instantly, then use your other ability - all while airborne for incredible aerial maneuvers!

Game Features

Momentum-Based Physics

Master slopes to build speed downhill and use your speed strategically.

Environmental Interaction

Navigate wind tunnels, dash through air rings, bounce off flowers, and use bridges strategically to maintain your flow through each level.

Living Towns & NPCs

Explore vibrant towns filled with characters offering dialogue, shops for upgrades, missions, and secrets to discover on your journey.

Multiplayer Races

Challenge friends to discover who is the fastest Windweaver. Compete in races across all levels.

Equippable Pins

Collect rare runes and bring them to the blacksmith to forge powerful pins, providing buffs and enhancements.

Ability Shard Collection

Discover ability shards throughout levels, each unlocking new movement options and combo possibilities for advanced play.
